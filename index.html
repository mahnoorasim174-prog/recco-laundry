The claim ticket

Four stops between your closet and ours.

Every order is tracked against its own ticket number, the same one printed on the tag stitched to your garment bag.

01

Book a Pickup

Choose a 30-minute window online or by phone. A driver arrives with your ticket already printed.

02

Sort & Clean

Garments are inspected, tagged to your ticket, and routed to the correct cycle for their fibre.

03

Press & Inspect

A second pair of eyes checks every seam and button before the garment is steamed and hung.

04

Deliver Back

Returned within your chosen window, bagged and matched to your original ticket number.
